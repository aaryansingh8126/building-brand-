1. Target Audience Strategy

Who to reach, where to find them, and exactly how to target them.

Primary Audience — Aspiring Parents

Who: Mothers aged 24–40 with children aged 2–15

  • Household income ₹6L–₹25L/year
  • Tier 1 cities: Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune
  • Tier 2: Jaipur, Lucknow, Chandigarh, Kochi, Ahmedabad
  • Mindset: Ambitious, want child to have "extra edge"
  • Already in: dance classes, hobby classes, school events
  • Scrolls Instagram for parenting, lifestyle, celeb kids

Secondary Audience — Aspirational Discoverers

Who: Fathers + grandparents aged 30–55

  • Influence purchasing decisions in joint families
  • Income: ₹10L+ (decision-makers)
  • Facebook-heavy (45+ age group)
  • Respond to: brand credibility, safety, ROI for child
  • Triggered by: "seen a child model on TV or ad"
  • Trust news articles, testimonials, brand logos

4. Lead Generation & Conversion System

Lead form, WhatsApp flow, and follow-up strategy to convert enquiries to office visits.

Ideal Lead Form Structure (Meta Native Form)

Use "Higher Intent" form type. Ask 5–7 questions to filter quality leads:

1
Child's name and age — Dropdown: 0–2, 3–5, 6–9, 10–15. Pre-fills from Facebook profile.
2
Your city — Delhi NCR / Mumbai / Bengaluru / Hyderabad / Other. Filters geography.
3
What are you looking for? — Modelling / Acting / Brand Shoots / All of the above. Qualifies intent.
4
Has your child done any modelling/acting before? — Yes / No. Segments beginner vs. experienced.
5
Phone number + WhatsApp number — Mandatory. Enables direct follow-up.
6
Best time to call? — Morning / Afternoon / Evening. Improves connect rate significantly.
7
Confirmation screen: "Thank you! Our team will reach out within 24 hours. Meanwhile, join our WhatsApp community." [Link]

WhatsApp Conversation Flow (Conversion Script)

GMN Team (within 5 min of lead)
Hi [Name]! 👋 I'm Priya from Green Media Network. I saw your enquiry about enrolling [child name] — that's wonderful! May I share a quick overview of our process?
Yes please!
Great! At GMN, we work with brands like Netflix, FirstCry, Amazon & more. Our process is: 1) Free application 2) Relaxed audition 3) Professional training 4) Brand bookings. No hidden fees. Can I ask — what drew you to GMN specifically?
I saw your Instagram. My daughter is 6 and loves posing!
That's perfect! 6 is a great age. We'd love to assess her potential. Can you share 2-3 casual photos of her — just natural shots, no studio needed? Our casting team will review within 48 hours. 📸
Also, we have a free orientation session this Saturday at our Noida office where you can meet the team and see our work. Would that work for you?

Key principles: respond within 5 min, personalise with child's name, create low-friction next step (photo → office visit).

Follow-Up Sequence (7 Days)

D0
Within 5 minutes: WhatsApp welcome message + intro PDF/brochure link. No sales pitch yet.
D1
Call attempt 1 at preferred time. If no answer, WhatsApp: "Hi [Name], tried calling — is there a better time?"
D2
Send social proof: Share 2 parent testimonial videos on WhatsApp. "Here's what other parents are saying."
D3
Call attempt 2. If connected: qualify, handle objections, pitch the office visit / audition slot.
D5
Retargeting ad activates — show testimonial ad to this contact via Custom Audience (phone number match).
D7
Final WhatsApp: "We have 2 open slots remaining for this month's batch in [city]. Would you like us to reserve one for [child name]?"

Ad Optimization Playbook

1
Week 1–2 (Learning phase): Run 3–5 ad variations per audience. Do NOT change anything. Let Meta's algorithm exit the learning phase (50 optimization events needed).
2
Week 3 (First cut): Kill ads with CTR below 0.8% or CPL 2x target. Pause underperforming audiences. Increase budget on winners by 20%.
3
Week 4+ (Scale): Duplicate winning ad sets. Increase budget max 20%/day. Introduce new creatives in a fresh ad set — never edit live winning ads.
4
Monthly creative refresh: Even winning creatives fatigue after 4–6 weeks. Rotate new hooks, new faces, new messaging angles. Keep the winning structure, change the content.
5
Frequency watch: When frequency exceeds 3.5 on Meta, CPL rises sharply. Expand audience or introduce new creatives immediately.

7. Scaling Strategy

From 5 to 50 leads/day — budget plan, creative testing, and growth milestones.

Phase-by-Phase Scaling Roadmap

→
5–10 leads/day. Budget: ₹500–800/day. Focus: 1 Meta campaign (Lead Gen), 1 Google Search campaign, manual WhatsApp follow-up. Identify 2–3 winning creatives.
Month 2
→
15–25 leads/day. Budget: ₹1,500/day. Add retargeting layer (MOFU), WhatsApp Click-to-Chat ads, test 2 new cities. Install CRM (Zoho/Leadsquared) to track follow-ups.
Month 3
→
30–40 leads/day. Budget: ₹3,000/day. Scale winning ad sets 20%/week. Add Google Display retargeting. Launch monthly "Open Audition" events to spike leads. Hire 1 dedicated sales caller.
Month 4–6
→
50+ leads/day. Budget: ₹5,000–8,000/day. Full 3-phase funnel live. Influencer partnerships (parenting influencers 50K–500K). Regional language ads (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu). Begin UAE/UK outreach.

Creative Testing Framework (Always-On)

Weekly testing structure

  • Test 3 hooks per week (first 3 seconds differ, rest same)
  • Test 2 CTAs per week ("Register free" vs "WhatsApp us")
  • Test 2 audience segments (interest vs LAL)
  • 1 winner declared after 5,000 impressions minimum
  • Winners: scale. Losers: archive (don't delete — data)

Video creative rules

  • Always show a real child (not stock/illustration)
  • First frame: child's face or emotional parent moment
  • Captions ON — 85% watch without sound
  • Under 30 seconds for Reels ads
  • End with 1 clear CTA + WhatsApp number on screen
